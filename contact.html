Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How far in advance should I book?

We recommend booking at least 1-2 weeks in advance for best availability. However, we also accommodate last-minute bookings when possible - just contact us!

Do you provide decorations for outdoor events?

Yes! We provide balloon decorations for both indoor and outdoor events. We use weather-appropriate materials for outdoor setups.

What is included in the service?

Our service includes consultation, custom design, all materials, setup at your venue, and cleanup after the event. Everything you need for a stress-free decoration experience!

How long do the decorations last?

Our balloon decorations typically last 12-24 hours in optimal conditions. We use high-quality balloons and proper inflation techniques to maximize longevity.
